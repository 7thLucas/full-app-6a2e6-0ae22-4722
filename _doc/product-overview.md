# Product Overview — Vibecart

## Product Identity
**Name**: Vibecart
**Category**: E-Commerce Marketing Web App & Website
**Tagline**: "Where Great Deals Meet a Premium Experience"
**Inspiration**: Flipkart · Amazon · Meesho

## Users
- **Online Shoppers** (Primary) — India's value-conscious middle class across metros and Tier-2/Tier-3 towns (and price-sensitive shoppers worldwide), seeking affordable, mid-range products and a frictionless, deal-led shopping experience — ideally in their own language — across Fashion, Electronics, Beauty, and Home Décor.
- **Marketing / Campaign Teams** (Secondary) — Brand operators who manage deal banners, sliding hero campaigns, and promotional discount launches to drive purchase intent and revenue.

## Problem
Online shoppers face cluttered, ad-heavy storefronts where the best deals are buried behind slow navigation and uninspiring UX. Brands invest in marketing campaigns but lose visibility inside visually mediocre product pages that fail to convert. Existing market leaders (Flipkart, Amazon, Meesho) are feature-rich but increasingly noisy — creating a clear gap for a leaner, app-grade alternative with superior UI polish.

## Solution
A modern, premium e-commerce marketing platform combining:
- The deal-discovery engine of **Flipkart**
- The comprehensive catalog depth of **Amazon**
- The accessible, social appeal of **Meesho**

…with superior UI polish, smooth animations, dark mode support, and an app-grade web experience on every device.

## Core Features (MVP)

| # | Feature | Description |
|---|---------|-------------|
| 1 | **Smart Top Bar** | Prominent search bar with voice search icon, shopping cart badge, user profile button — always accessible |
| 2 | **Hero Deal Carousel** | Auto-sliding full-width banners — live deals (e.g. "50% OFF"), trending campaigns, new arrival launches |
| 3 | **Category Discovery Grid** | Horizontally scrollable row of rounded icons: Fashion · Electronics · Beauty · Home Décor · and more |
| 4 | **Product Showcase Grid** | Cards with: hi-res image · title · short description · current price · strikethrough original price · bold discount % tag · rating badge (e.g. 4.5 ★) · "Add to Cart" button |
| 5 | **Sticky Bottom Navigation** | Fixed app-style bar — Home · Categories · My Cart · Orders · Profile |
| 6 | **Dark Mode Toggle** | Premium light/dark theme switch for extended, eye-friendly browsing sessions |

## Design Language
- **Color Palette**: Vibrant yet professional — deep indigo/navy primary, orange accent for deal highlights, rose/coral for sale badges (Amazon/Meesho-inspired)
- **Motion**: Smooth carousel auto-play, animated screen transitions, responsive button interactions
- **Typography**: Bold display type for prices and deal tags; clean, legible body text for product details
- **Layout**: Mobile-first, app-grade — sticky header, sticky bottom nav, fluid product grid, safe inner padding throughout

## Brand & Tone
Premium, modern, and conversion-focused — yet built for the mass market. Earns the visual authority of top-tier e-commerce platforms while staying genuinely affordable and accessible for India's middle class, with the inclusivity of regional-language support (Meesho-style reach, Amazon-grade polish).

## Phase 1 Scope (MVP)
- Full home page with all 6 UI sections (Top Bar, Hero Carousel, Category Grid, Product Grid, Bottom Nav, Dark Mode)
- Static product catalog across Fashion, Electronics, Beauty, Home Décor — with deal badges and star ratings
- Add to Cart interaction
- Dark mode toggle
- Mobile-optimized, app-style layout
